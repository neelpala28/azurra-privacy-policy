Azurra – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Azurra ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Azurra mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

By using Azurra, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account in Azurra, we may collect information such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Profile information
* Profile picture (if provided)

This information is used to create and manage your user account.

### User Content

Azurra allows users to create and interact with content including:

* Posts
* Likes
* Profile information
* Other interactions within the app

This content may be visible to other users of the application.

### Usage Data

We may automatically collect certain technical information such as:

* Device type
* App usage data
* Log information

This information helps us improve app performance and user experience.

## Third-Party Services

Azurra uses third-party services to operate the application.

### Firebase (Google)

Azurra uses Google Firebase services, including:

* Firebase Authentication (for user login)
* Cloud Firestore (for storing user data and app content)

These services may collect certain technical information as required to operate the platform.

You can read Google's privacy policy here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Information

The information we collect may be used to:

* Provide and maintain the Azurra application
* Enable user authentication
* Store and display user-generated content
* Improve app performance and functionality
* Monitor usage and prevent abuse or misuse

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared:

* With trusted service providers such as Firebase to operate the app
* If required by law or legal obligation

## Data Retention

User data is stored as long as the user maintains an account on Azurra.

If an account is deleted, associated personal data will be removed from our systems except where retention is required for legal or security purposes.

## Account Deletion

Azurra allows users to permanently delete their account directly within the app.

When a user deletes their account, associated personal information and user-generated content will be permanently removed from our systems.

If users experience issues deleting their account through the app, they may contact us for assistance.

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Azurra is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to remove that information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the updated date.
