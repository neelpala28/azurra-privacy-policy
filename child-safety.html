Azurra Child Safety Standards

Azurra is committed to providing a safe and respectful platform for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and strictly prohibit any content that involves or promotes such behavior.

Prohibited Content

  • Any content involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Content that endangers the safety of children

User Reporting

Users can report harmful content or users directly through the Azurra application. Reported content will be reviewed and appropriate actions will be taken, including removal of content and suspension of accounts.

Compliance

Azurra complies with applicable child safety laws and works with relevant authorities when required to address reports involving child exploitation or abuse.
